Arka Energy Iranian (AEI) was formed in 2010 to provide complete engineering design, and procurement services to the oil & gas and petrochemical industries. At AEI, by relying on our expert personnel, the vast experience of its founders in the oil and gas industries which extends for more than a decade, advanced software and hardware, technical expertise and up-to-date management techniques, we offer engineering, and consultancy services for Onshore and offshore oil and gas facilities.

AEI managers have had a long experience in the Oil and Gas field for nearly twenty years where they began their carriers in other companies as design engineers, installation managers etc. and since 2010 by establishing AEI they combined all their past experiences in form of a single company to satisfy the needs of their customers who mainly include end users, and EPC contractors in the Oil & Gas field.

With a team of experts always at hand AEI thrives to offer its best services in the field of Engineering Services from A to Z to all the demands of the customers for every inquiry they have. Our consultancy approach is aimed at having a win-win strategy where all parties involved are satisfied in all circumstances, and in the field of equipment we continue our services to installation, commissioning, and after sales services for all the items we supply.

Engineering Services offered by AEI range from Conceptual Design, feasibility study, up to Procurement Services which includes technical assistance in form of having technical meetings with the customers to understand their engineering requirements in the contractual phase, performing technical evaluations to understand the scale of technical and engineering services they require and making sure that our offered equipment, or service exactly matches their requirement.

AEI is also a leading supplier of equipment to the Oil & Gas Industries in Iran. The main equipment supplied by AEI include Shell & Tube Heat Exchangers, Plate Heat Exchangers, Reactors, Pressure Vessels, Different Types of Valves, Specialty Gases, Off Gas Treatment Packages, Instrumentation, and Cryogenic equipment.
